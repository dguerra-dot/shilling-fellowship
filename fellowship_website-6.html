Great companies are built by the Portuguese.

But some of our best minds still don't pursue this path.

We want to change that by nurturing the next generation of founders, technologists and investors.

To that end, we are launching the Shilling Fellowship.

A 10 week, €10.000, summer program for college students (or dropouts) with a deep interest in technology and exceptional ability.

Application Deadline March 13, 2026
Interviews begin March 16, 2026
Fellow Selected March 27, 2026
Apply now
Who's eligible?
There's no strict age limit, but we're looking for individuals early in their career. So people still in college, or college age are more likely to get in.
Do I need to be in a business degree?
No. We want to attract engineers, researchers, biologists, product thinkers, and more.
Can I do it remote?
No. The fellowship is to be done in person at our offices in Lisbon. We believe it is how you will learn the fastest.
When does it start and end?
We want the fellow to be full time with us, so we will adapt the dates slightly to ensure you don't have exams at the same time. But it should start around the first week of June and end in the middle of August.
If I'm not from Lisbon how can I afford the stay?
We want to give the same chance to everyone, so the €10,000 will be paid upfront so that you can arrange transport and accommodation.
